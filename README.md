The main objective of this project on Online E-Nursery System is 
to build an application program that reduces the manual work for 
E-Nursery and for managing the details about the customer, 
product, address, order, stock, delivery, payment. 
Nowadays E-commerce websites have become very convenient to 
purchase products and services online. Online E-nursery is a form 
of E-commerce that enables Sellers and Costumers to directly buy 
fresh and colorful plants over the internet. Customers can 
purchase products through 24/7 service comfortably and 
conveniently.
